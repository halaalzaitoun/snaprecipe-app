# SnapRecipe

**Turn saved recipes into something you’ll actually use.**

SnapRecipe is a product designed to help users turn messy recipe content from screenshots, social media, and notes into a clean, searchable recipe library.

---

## 🌍 Live Demo

App: https://snaprecipe-app.lovable.app/

---

## 💡 The Problem

Recipes today are scattered across different sources:

* screenshots saved on phones
* Instagram posts and reels
* notes and bookmarks

This makes it difficult to:

* find recipes later
* follow them easily
* compare or organize them
* understand nutritional value

As a result, many saved recipes are never actually used.

---

## 🎯 The Solution

SnapRecipe transforms unstructured recipe content into clean, usable recipes.

Users can:

* import recipes from screenshots, links, or text
* review and edit extracted ingredients and instructions
* see estimated calories and macros
* save and organize recipes into collections

The goal is to turn recipe inspiration into something **practical and reusable**.

---

## 🧭 Product Concept

SnapRecipe is built around a simple transformation:

### 1. Capture (Messy Input)

Users import recipes from:

* screenshots
* captions
* links
* manual entry

---

### 2. Structure (Parsing & Review)

The app converts unstructured content into:

* ingredients
* instructions
* tags
* nutrition summary

Users can review and edit before saving.

---

### 3. Use (Organized Library)

Recipes are saved into a clean, searchable library.

Users can:

* filter by tags (high protein, dinner, dessert)
* organize into collections
* quickly access recipes when cooking

---

## ✨ MVP Features

* recipe import (image, link, or text)
* parsing and review flow
* editable recipe details
* nutrition summary (mock)
* recipe library with filters
* collections for organization

---

## 🔮 Future Direction

SnapRecipe can evolve into a broader food and planning platform:

* meal planning
* grocery list generation
* AI ingredient substitutions
* dietary filtering (vegan, gluten-free, high protein)
* duplicate recipe detection
* recipe recommendations

---

## 🧪 Product Thinking Highlights

This project explores:

* transforming **unstructured input → structured data**
* reducing friction between **saving and actually using content**
* designing for real-world behavior (saving vs cooking)
* rapid product prototyping using AI tools
